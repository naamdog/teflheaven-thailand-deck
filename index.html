Are You Eligible?

A short, honest checklist. Tick five — you're in.

Can I Apply?

The 5-box checklist.

🎂21 to 55 years old.  56–59 case-by-case.
🎓A Bachelor's degree.  Any subject. Required for the work permit.
📜A 120-hour accredited TEFL.  Included if you don't have one.
🛂Eligible passport.  USA · CA · UK · IE · AU · NZ · ZA (others case-by-case)
🔒A clean criminal record check.  FBI / ACRO / RCMP / etc.

Don't Count Yourself Out

The things people wrongly rule themselves out for.

🎨

Tattoos.

Small visible tattoos are usually fine. Only Buddha imagery is a firm no (out of respect).

👶

No experience.

Most of our teachers have never taught. The TESOL is built exactly for that.

🧕

Religious dress.

Hijabs and similar reviewed case-by-case. Flexibility on placement helps.

💑

Family / pets.

Possible case-by-case for partners. You cover all costs and logistics for them.

If anything is a "maybe", just ask. We've seen it before.

What People Worry About

Honest answers.

To the things you're not asking out loud.

"I've never taught before."

That's what the 4-week TESOL is for. Most of our teachers had never taught before they signed up.

"Is Thailand safe?"

One of the safest places to teach abroad. You arrive in a cohort, in-country team, 24/7 line.

"What if I'm lonely?"

You arrive with 15–30 other teachers. WhatsApp groups don't go quiet for years.

"What if I hate it?"

Placement guarantee means we move you. If it's really not for you, you leave with a Trinity-tier TEFL.

"Can I really afford this?"

With Earn-Back, many cover their costs before they fly. The maths gets friendly fast.

"What about my partner?"

Possible case-by-case. Most couples come together and find adjacent placements.

An Honest Filter

This programme is not for everyone.

It's not for people who think they're going on a year-long holiday. You will teach 25–35 small humans English on Tuesday morning when you're tired and possibly hungover.

It's not for people who want guarantees we can't make. Schools are real schools with real expectations. Some weeks will be hard.

It's not for people who already had it all figured out. If you don't need this, don't apply.

It is for people who suspect there's a different life available, and who'll take twelve months to find out.
