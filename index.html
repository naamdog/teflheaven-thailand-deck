Part Four · Are You Eligible?24 / 44
Don't Count Yourself Out

The things people wrongly rule themselves out for.

Read this carefully. You're probably more eligible than you think.

Tattoos.

Small visible tattoos are usually fine — tell us at signup. The only firm no is Buddha imagery (out of respect — it's actually illegal to display in some contexts in Thailand).

No teaching experience.

Most of our teachers have never taught before. The TESOL is built exactly for that. Eighteen years of doing this; the methodology is sound.

Religious dress.

Hijabs and similar are reviewed case-by-case. Flexibility on placement helps a lot — we've placed many. Talk to us honestly.

Family or pets.

Possible case-by-case for partners and dependents (you cover all costs and logistics for them). Most couples come together and find adjacent placements.

Health conditions.

Most are fine. Some (epilepsy, anxiety with meds, depression with meds) need a doctor's note. We'll talk it through honestly and tell you up front what's workable.

Old criminal offences.

Minor things from years ago are reviewed case-by-case. Honesty up front is the only thing that matters.

If anything is a "maybe", just ask. We've seen it before.

What People Worry About

Honest answers.

To the things you're not asking out loud.

"I've never taught before. Am I qualified?"

That's exactly what the 4-week TESOL is for. You'll learn to plan a lesson, run a classroom, and teach English from zero. Most of our teachers had never taught before they signed up.

"Is Thailand safe?"

Thailand is one of the safest places to teach abroad. You arrive in a cohort, you're supported by our in-country team, and we're a phone call away 24/7.

"What if I'm lonely?"

You arrive with 15–30 other teachers in your cohort. By the end of week one, you'll have lifelong friends. The TEFL Heaven WhatsApp groups don't go quiet for years.

"What if I hate it?"

First, you probably won't. But if something's wrong, our placement guarantee means we move you. If it's really not for you, you leave with a Trinity-tier TEFL and a story worth telling.

"Can I really afford this?"

With our Earn-Back digital-marketing programme, many teachers cover a chunk of their costs before they fly. Once you're earning ฿30–฿50k with rent at ฿5–฿15k, the maths gets very friendly very fast.

"What about my partner / pets?"

Both are possible case-by-case. You cover all costs and logistics for them — programme support stays focused on you. Most couples come together and find adjacent placements.

An Honest Filter

This programme is not for everyone.

It's not for people who think they're going on a year-long holiday. We will not hand you a beach with a paycheck attached. You will teach 25–35 small humans English on Tuesday morning when you're tired, sweating, and possibly hungover.

It's not for people who want guarantees we can't make. Schools are real schools with real expectations. Some weeks will be hard. Some classes will not love you.

It's not for people who already had it all figured out. If you don't need this, don't apply.

It is for people who suspect there's a different life available, and who are willing to take twelve months to find out.
